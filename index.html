Optimal Hedging with Advanced Delta Modelling

This project explores volatility arbitrage opportunities arising from options mispricing. Monte Carlo simulations demonstrate the profit distribution when delta-hedging under actual versus implied volatility. The analysis also includes a practical implementation of the Minimum Variance Delta model proposed by Hull and White.
